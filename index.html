Welcome ADDRESS

Dear colleagues, dear aviators, dear friends:

On behalf of the Spanish Air and Space Chief of Staff, as Commander of San Javier Air Base, home of the Air and Space Force Academy, receive my warmest welcome to this unique location nearby the Mar Menor. Welcome to “Aire-25”, the greatest airshow to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the Spanish acrobatic team “Patrulla Águila”. An incredible four decades have flown by since a group of courageous fighter pilots created this team in 1985 and started to perform airshows all over the world and became Spanish ambassadors painting the skies with our national flag. During this special weekend it’s time to recognize these heroes and share with them a remarkable celebration accompanied with other sister acrobatic teams, many aviators and general public from everywhere.

This “Aire-25” airshow has been organized by the Spanish Air and Space Force, as a whole, with the support of the surrounding towns of San Javier, San Pedro del Pinatar, Los Alcázares and also with the support of local and regional authorities, and it will be seen by half a million people around the beaches of Mar Menor. It’s going to be a blast!!

Along with this Display Order, we have enclosed all the necessary information and procedures needed to safely and efficiently participate in “Aire-25”. If you have any further questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to contact your designated POC.

I look forward to saluting you in person, wishing you a successful and safe Aire-25 airshow and a joyful stay in this beautiful Murcia region.

Col. Luis Felipe González Asenjo

Spanish Air Force and Space Academy Director

SCOPE

The aim of this Aircrew guide is to establish common procedures and information to all participants in order to obtain safe and expeditious air operations throughout the Patrulla Águila´s 40th Anniversary Air Show (Aire 25).

Air operations comprise arrivals and departures of all aircraft, including supporting aircraft, as well as static, rehearsal and display flights.

The guide has been divided in four main sections:

ADMIN
In this section you will find details about diplomatic clearance, arrival and departure admin, general facility layout, etc.

AIRBASE OPS
This section provides all approach and departure charts for San Javier as well as the main alternates during the Air Show.

DISPLAY ORDER
This section guides you through the regulations, limitations and instructions that each participant shall comply with during the Air Show.

Diplomatic-clearance

For all the participants to the Airshow (static, display and support), a Diplomatic Clearance is needed.

Check-in

Upon your arrival at SAN JAVIER AB, you will be led to the In Processing Desk for the check-in.

There you will get a welcome package with ID passes and all the admin details (accommodation, catering, transportation, Flying schedule, etc.). There, you will meet the Liaison Officer assigned to your unit for any support you may need.

The Current Ops Desk will be your FOCAL POINT during your staying in San Javier ready to fulfill your requests!

Accomodation

Air and ground crew personnel will be accommodated in hotels at Los Alcázares, San Javier, Santiago de la Ribera or San Pedro, all inside a 10 km circle from San Javier Airbase. Hotel accommodation will mainly be in single rooms for Officers and double rooms for NCOs. The organization will cover accommodation and breakfast costs, from Thursday/Friday to Monday. All extras shall be paid by each single participant.

Facility overview meals

Breakfast will be available at your hotel. On-base cafeteria is also available. Lunch will be available, free of charge on base from Thursday 12th to Sunday 15th June. Dinner on your own.

SECURITY/FORCE PROTECTION

Aircrew and Ground personnel must wear visible at all times their personal badge. Individuals shall remain vigilant and alert for possible threats at all times, especially when off base. Any suspicious activity must be reported to military police (Ext. 2314). Or +34 968 189 314 if you call from outside. Force Protection and access control will be active all the time to ensure Air base security. Night-view camera surveillance system will be active. Crypto equipment and sensitive material may be stored into appropriate designated facility.

INSURANCE

The organization will contract liability insurance for Saturday 14th and Sunday 15th June. Participants shall produce proof of a valid third party liability insurance covering all flight and ground operations carried out by Units during the deployment at San Javier AB.
